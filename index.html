Who I Am

I’ve always been connected to communication, and to the power of words to shape perception and move things forward.

I began my career in PR and strategic communications, working in environments where clarity, positioning and timing truly mattered.

Over time, I became curious not only about what we say, but about what happens behind the scenes in the brain - how messages are processed, what captures attention, what shapes perception, and more broadly, how the brain works.

That curiosity led me to study neuroscience and psychology alongside my professional path.

Today, I work in tech marketing, looking at the big picture of how complex stories - hardware, software, and AI - come together, and turning them into content people actually want to engage with.

From messages that cut through attention to visuals that make you stay a little longer, I work end to end to create stories that genuinely connects.
